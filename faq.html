Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tea Token?

TeaToken is an utility token compliant with Ethereum's ERC20 Token Standard which helps to facilitate transactions between Tea bussinesses and Tea customers all over the world through the TeaBay platform. The TeaBay platform will be a marketplace in which farmers, bussiness owners and tea consumers will be able to trade products and services.

Are TeaTokens transferable? Can I trade them?

The main purporse of the Token is meant to help fuel transactions within the TeaBay platform. Although not the main purpose, the token can be traded outside the TeaBay platform.

What do TeaToken represent?

As an utility Token it represents a mean of interaction between users of the TeaToken platform. Ownership of the tokens represents no more than the right to participate in the TeaToken platform. The tokens are not intended to be a security, commodity or any other kind of financial instrument.

How do I view my TeaToken Balance?

As an ERC20 Standard Token, you can view your token balance in any Ethereum Wallet with ERC20 Tokens support.

Which wallets can be used to store TeaTokens?

TeaToken is an ERC20 Token and can be stored in any Ethereum ERC20 compatible wallets. The most popular on Ethereum are Ledger Nano S hardware wallet and MyEtherWallet online Wallet.
